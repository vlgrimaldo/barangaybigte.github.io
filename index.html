Bigte is a barangay in the municipality of Norzagaray, Bulacan province. According to the 2020 Census, its population was 15,119. This accounted for 11.11% of Norzagaray's total population.

The barangay Bigte household population was 12,118 in 2015, divided into 2,667 households with an average of 4.54 persons per family.
